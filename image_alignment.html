Exercise 4: Creating Clickable Images with Image Maps

Image Maps

Click on the computer, or the coffe, or the ipad, to go to a new page and read more about the topic:

Code

                <img src="/images/ex4.jpg" alt="Workplace" usemap="#workmap" width="400" height="379">

                <map name="workmap">
                    <area shape="rect" coords="150,140,250,240" alt="Computer" href="https://www.apple.com/es/mac/compare/">
                    <area shape="rect" coords="0,250,100,150" alt="Computer" href="https://www.apple.com/es/ipad-pro/">

                    <area shape="circle" coords="380,100,40" alt="Cup of coffee" href="https://www.starbucks.es/">
                </map>
